Back in 2013, I started as a game programmer for the 3D Action-Adventure video game "Crying Is Not Enough".

I built most of the mechanics of the game using Javascript and C# and dealt with UI, Physics, 3Ds, Animations, etc.

In 2015 I started a 2-year "Computer Science" program in "Public Vocational Training Institute" and expanded my knowledge around computers and programming.

In 2019 I started my own gym. One of my main tasks was to build and maintain a fully functional website that would make our lives and the users' lives easier. I created a wordpress page that had a lot of features such as online booking, a subscription system, and a lot of automation systems that replaced the time-consuming tasks. Anyone could do anything without a call. The users could book an appointment, buy a subscription online, learn everything about our programs and pass all the data that we needed without extra work for the receptionist. Our website was one of the reasons for success!

In 2021 I started an educational-oriented online business named "Calisthenics Association". I created a subscription-based online platform with courses, the same concept as Udemy and Coursera, but for specific content. Today managing only online businesses, I am more focused on front-end web development, which is something that I love. By automating my work, I reserve a good amount of time to keep learning new technologies around web development. I enjoy creating web applications in Javascript and ReactJ, using HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap. I am passionate about web development. I am willing to engage in new projects, offer my knowledge, and get more experience by collaborating with the members of a team!
